# Runestone Keyboard Shortcuts

## Editor View

- Find, ⌘ F
- Find and Replace, ⌥ ⌘ F
- Find Next, ⌘ G
- Find Previous, .⇧ ⌘ G
- Go to Line, ^ G
- Shift Left, ⌘ [
- Shift Right, ⌘ ]
- Move Lines Up, ⌥ ↑
- Move Lines Down, ⌥ ↓
- Increase Font Size, ⌘ +
- Decrease Font Size, ⌘ -
- Settings, ⌘ ,
- Close, ⌘ W
- Print, ⌘ P
