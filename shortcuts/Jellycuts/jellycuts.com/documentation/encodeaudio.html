Encode Audio

Notes

Encodes the given audio.

Syntax

encodeAudio(media: <#Variable#>, format: <#Type (WFMediaAudioFormat)#>, speed: <#Type (WFMediaSpeed)#>, customSpeed: <#String (Allows Variables)#>, title: <#String (Allows Variables)#>, artist: <#String (Allows Variables)#>, album: <#String (Allows Variables)#>, genre: <#String (Allows Variables)#>, year: <#Integer or Variable#>, artwork: <#Variable#>)

Example

encodeAudio(media: Shortcut Input, format: M4A, speed: Normal, customSpeed: "0", title: "Awesome Audio", artist: "Zachary", album: "Music", genre: "Indie", year: 2021, artwork: Image)
